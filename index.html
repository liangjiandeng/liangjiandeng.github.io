Xueyang Fu  傅雪阳

    Ph.D.

    University of Science and Technology of China, Anhui, China

    xyfu@ustc.edu.cn

I am an associate researcher in the Department of Automation at USTC (中国科学技术大学).

In 2018, I received my Ph.D. in Signal and Information Processing from Xiamen University (厦门大学), advised by Prof. Xinghao Ding (丁兴号 教授).

From Dec. 2016 to Dec. 2017, I was a visiting Ph.D. student, sponsored by the China Scholarship Council, at Columbia University, working there with Prof. John Paisley.

My research interest includes Computer Vision and Machine Learning.
