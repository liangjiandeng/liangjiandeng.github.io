I am an associate professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences of UESTC (电子科技大学).

I received B.S. and Ph.D. degrees (supervised by Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang) from the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), in 2010 and 2016, respectively. From Sept. 2013 to Sept. 2014, I followed Prof. Weihong Guo at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU), Cleveland, USA, as a Joint-Training Ph.D. student. In 2017, I was as a postdoc. by prof. Xue-Cheng Tai in HKBU. My research interest is mainly including image processing and computer vision, including fusion, restoration, super-resolution, etc.
