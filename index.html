    Email: liangjian.deng@uestc.edu.cn

    Office: Innovation Center A-401, Qingshuihe Campus, Chengdu, Sichuan, China

I am a professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences of UESTC (电子科技大学).

I was born in Xichang, a small city in Liangshan Prefecture of Sichuan Province. In 2010 and 2016, I got my B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), under the supervision of Prof. Ting-Zhu Huang. As a Joint-Training Ph.D. student, I worked with Prof. Weihong Guo at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU) in Cleveland, Ohio, from September 2013 to September 2014. In 2017, I was a postdoctoral researcher at HKBU under Prof. Xue-Cheng Tai (Hong Kong, China). I also paid short visits to the Isaac Newton Institute for Mathematical Sciences (University of Cambridge) and HKBU. My research interest is mainly focusing on using PDE, Optimization modeling and Deep learning to address several tasks in image processing and computer vision, e.g., resolution enhancement, restoration, etc.
